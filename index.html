Refreshing summer drink recepie

Iced Jaljeera

Ingredients

  • 125 gram tamarind pulp
  • 3 tbsp mint leaves
  • 1/2 tsp ground cumin
  • 3/4 ground cumin,roasted
  • 50 gram jaggery,grated
  • 4 tsp black salt
  • 1/2 liter of water

How to make

  1. Add all ingredient in food processor and blend it
  2. chill overnight.Then strain and freeze it
  3. garnish the drink with some boondis and serve
